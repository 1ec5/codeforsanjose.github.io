At Code for San José, civic tech means so much more than helping communities understand and navigate local government.

It also means working harder than ever to reach more people and communities.

Donate Now!

Your generous gift will support our renewed efforts to connect to all of San José through hosting biweekly community meetings, as well as events like the National Day of Civic Hacking. Your donation funds:

  • Food we offer our volunteers at our weekly community meetings
  • Name tags, paper pads and other basic materials used at our meetings
  • Hosting for our projects’ Web sites
  • Business cards and Stickers
  • Basic resources used for annual events like National Day of Civic Hacking and other outreach activities

The Donate button will take you to Code for America’s Donate page where Code for San José will be pre-populated to make sure the funds are earmarked for us.

Thank you!
